Browser is a Shelter plugin that lets you browse and manage your installed plugins and themes directly within the app.

Features:

  • View all installed plugins in one place
  • Browse available plugins and themes
  • Quick enable/disable toggles
  • Access plugin settings easily

How to use:

Open the Plugins or Themes section from the discord settings sidebar to view your plugins and themes. Then you can enable or disable them from there or discover new ones.
